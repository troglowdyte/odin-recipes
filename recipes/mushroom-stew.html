Recipe 1

Description

The savior of many cave explorers and wanderers, this hearty mushroom stew will keep your belly filled while reminding you of thos faraway mushroom lands and mooshrooms. To make this delicious stew, you will need to gather mushrooms and have a bowl handy.

Ingredients

  • 1 Red mushroom
  • 1 Brown mushroom
  • 1 Bowl

Steps

  1. Add brown mushroom to crafting table.
  2. Add red mushroom to crafting table.
  3. Add bowl to crafting table.
  4. voila!
