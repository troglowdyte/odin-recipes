Recipe 2

Description

Delicious, fluffy, and decadent. This cake will have you drooling and wanting more and more until it is all gone. However, you can't have your cake and eat it too.

Ingredients

  • 3 Wheat
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 Milk buckets
  • 2 Sugars

Steps

  1. Fill bottom row of crafting table with the 3 wheat, one in each square.
  2. Fill top row of crafting table with the 3 milk buckets, one in each square.
  3. Place the egg in the centermost square of the crafting table.
  4. Fill the squares to the left and the right of the egg with 1 sugar each.
  5. Voila! You have cake now.
